SECTION 1 - WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 09:00-17:00, Monday through Friday. Flexible arrangements
may be requested through your line manager.

SECTION 2 - EXPENSES
All expenses over $50 require a receipt and manager approval within 30 days.

SECTION 3 - SECURITY POLICY
Employees must rotate their passwords every 90 days and enable multi-factor
authentication on all corporate accounts.
